# Let's say ...

```
I am prepared for the worst, but hope for the best. -- Benjamin Disraeli
我们期待最好的，但是为最坏的做好准备。0
```

```
say/let's say ... 用作插入语，表示比如，例如，比如说。
等于 for example
```

```
Let's forget about work and do something fun, say, catch a movie.
我们别想工作，做点好玩的事吧，比如，看个电影。

Besides watching TV, you have a lots of other things to do in your spare time, say, reading.
除了看电视，你的空余时间还可以做很多事情，比如，看看书。

How about we go shopping tomorrow. Let's say, 10 in the morning?
明天我们去逛街怎么样？早上 10 点？
```
